<p><strong>The Anti-Reflux Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive Anti-Reflux Device</li><li>Non-invasive Anti-Reflux Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-reflux device market is divided into two main types: invasive and non-invasive devices. Invasive anti-reflux devices typically involve surgical implantation to prevent acid reflux by creating a physical barrier in the lower esophagus. These devices aim to provide long-term relief for patients with severe symptoms. Conversely, non-invasive anti-reflux devices include wearable or external solutions that manage reflux symptoms without surgery, often using lifestyle modifications or mechanical adjustments to reduce acid exposure, making them suitable for milder cases.</p></p>
