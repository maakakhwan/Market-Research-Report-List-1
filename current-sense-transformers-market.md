<p><ul><li>Mutual Inductance Measuring Current</li><li>Protective Current Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Current Sense Transformers market comprises two main types: mutual inductance measuring current transformers and protective current transformers. Mutual inductance measuring current transformers are designed for accurate current measurement in electrical systems, transferring information through inductive coupling. In contrast, protective current transformers focus on safeguarding equipment by monitoring and isolating fault conditions, thereby preventing damage. Both types are essential for ensuring reliability, efficiency, and safety in power distribution and industrial applications, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
