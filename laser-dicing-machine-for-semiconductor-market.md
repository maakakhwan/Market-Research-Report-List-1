<p><p>Laser dicing machines for the semiconductor market are crucial for precise cutting of silicon wafers. There are two primary types: laser ablation dicing and stealth dicing. Laser ablation dicing uses focused laser beams to remove material, creating cuts without mechanical stress. Stealth dicing, on the other hand, employs a more advanced technique where lasers generate internal micro-cracks, allowing for cleaner and finer cuts without damaging adjacent layers. Both methods enhance manufacturing efficiency and yield in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
