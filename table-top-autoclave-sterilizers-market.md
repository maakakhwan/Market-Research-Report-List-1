<p><p>Tabletop autoclave sterilizers are categorized into Class B, Class N, and Class S based on their sterilization capabilities and application. Class B autoclaves are versatile, suitable for sterilizing wrapped instruments, porous loads, and biological materials, making them ideal for healthcare settings. Class N autoclaves primarily sterilize unwrapped instruments and are often used in dental offices. Class S models offer a more limited function, best suited for sterilizing small, wrapped loads in specific applications, such as labs or small clinics.</p></p>
